A Passion for Food That Started It All

Modern Food Express is a unique restaurant in Sufinagar, Bhadohi — perfectly located on NH-2 G.T. Road at the Modern Filling Station. We serve South Indian, Chinese, Indian, and Continental food with a style of service that makes everyone feel welcome and special.

Our food products are of the finest quality, prepared with sensitivity and care. We go all the way to satisfy our guests. The graceful ambience, coupled with delicious and pure vegetarian food, is the perfect combination for all food lovers visiting Bhadohi.
